Line 'Em Up

Summary:

Line 'Em Up was developed over the course of the Spring 2019 semester for the TAGD semester game jam. I worked with a group of three other guys to complete this project which ultimately won us second place at the jam.

Concept:

Line 'Em Up is a 2D top-down shooter, but unlike most, the best way to elimate your oppenents isn't actually to shoot them, it is instead to hurl yourself at your teammate at incredible speeds in order to smash any enemies that may be in the way.

What I did:

As the lead programmer for the project I was responsible for implementing the majority of the game mechanics, which dealt with shooting, player physics, match system, and consumables.
