[Raylib] Rendering textures in world space

August 13, 2024

Here we will discuss how to render textures in world space with a 2D camera in Raylib. By default, texture rendering is done in screen space, meaning you specify sizes and positions in pixels. This is fine for some applications, but in a lot of cases you do not want to be forced to adhere to pixel boundaries, and using arbitrary coordinates (world coordinates) is very valuable as it is not dependent on the size of the screen.
